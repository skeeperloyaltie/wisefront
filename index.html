Our Programs

CBC (Play Group to Grade 9)

Our Competency-Based Curriculum focuses on practical skills, critical thinking, and holistic development from Play Group to Grade 9. Students engage in hands-on learning, projects, and assessments tailored to their competencies.

Junior Secondary School (JSS)

JSS (Grades 7-9) builds on CBC, preparing students for Senior Secondary with a focus on specialized subjects, career pathways, and practical skills. Our program emphasizes innovation and leadership.

8-4-4 Transition Support

We offer bridging programs for students transitioning from the 8-4-4 system to CBC, ensuring they adapt to competency-based learning while meeting national standards.
